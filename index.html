What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing your website to get better visibility in search engines like Google, Bing, and others. Good SEO brings in organic traffic and builds long-term visibility. It's not just about keywords ‚Äî it's about speed, structure, mobile friendliness, and high-quality content.

SEO & Content Strategy

Content is king. Search engines prioritize websites that provide users with relevant, useful, and well-structured information. Consistency, clarity, and proper keyword usage are vital for rankings.

  • Write for people, not just algorithms
  • Use headers (H1-H3) to structure your content
  • Include internal and external links naturally
  • Optimize images with ALT text and compression

SEO & Performance

Performance optimization directly impacts SEO. Google favors sites that are fast, responsive, and accessible.

  • Minimize CSS and JS files
  • Enable lazy loading for images
  • Use browser caching and CDN
  • Implement responsive design for all devices
